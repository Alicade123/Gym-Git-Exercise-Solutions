Requirements for applicants

We expect you to bring:
  • Relentless passion for software development.
  • Willingness to invest a very high volume of time and energy into building your skills as a developer.
  • Willingness to embrace open and direct communication paradigms, a detailed approach to task-management and a high standard of reliability and quality of work.
  • Strong foundations in software development. These could have been acquired through a bootcamp (e.g. Microverse, SheCanCode, kLab) or a similar program (e.g. Andela Technical Leadership Program) or in self-study and practical project work.
  • Very good English skills. You need to be able to explain and understand technical concepts in English with ease. You need to be able to read technical documentations and tech-related articles, write clear messages with technical updates and compose documentation notes.
